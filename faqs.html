Getting Started

  • What is UniHive?
    UniHive is a 100% free social platform for Black and people of color MBA enthusiasts at all stages in the process from prospective students to current students to alumni. Members can post/answer questions, start discussions, do pulse-checks and get connected with fellow peers!
  • How do I join UniHive?
    You can sign up for UniHive here via computer, laptop, tablet, or phone! An email address is required.
  • How do I navigate the UniHive platform?
    Your Feed Your feed is a central place of activity on UniHive, just like a timeline! From here, you can:
    • Create posts
    • Answer questions
    • Interact with posts from other UniHive members
    Your feed allows you to post your thoughts to the main feed which sends it to all UniHive members or to your specific Communities. You choose! You can also add topics to your post or question so UniHive members can follow that specific discussion.
    Your Profile This is your space to share what you want the Hive to know about you!
    Communities When you join UniHive, you will automatically become a member of the Hive. In true bee fashion, this is where you will find every member on the platform. You can access the Hive on the left side bar on Your Feed. Feel free to write a message introducing yourself to the Hive and you will be greeted right back! You can create a post or as a question using the [post box] at the center of the screen. The Hive is a quick way to start connecting with people or just to see what types of discussions are happening! Whether you are a silent bee or a busy one, we are excited to have you here!
    Search Looking for one of your UniHive friends really quickly? Are you trying to find a post from earlier in the year about the best GRE books? UniHive’s search bar is resourceful. Type in words
    Messaging You can send direct messages to UniHive members by clicking this icon to the right of the search bar. Type and click a member's name in the search bar on the left and start writing your message at the bottom of your screen! Do not worry about whether or not you are following the person you are messaging. You have the option!
    Once you write your message, click that member’s icon. A screen will pop up on the right side of the screen and you can view how long that member has been on UniHive and choose to follow them if you want!

Your Profile

  • How do I get to my profile?
    You can find your profile by clicking your profile picture in the top right corner and navigating to Your Profile.
  • How can I change my email address on my account?
    You can change your email address by clicking your profile picture in the top right corner and navigating to Account Settings. Under Account, you will see Email and your current email address. Type in the new email address and click Update Email.
  • How can I change my name, profile picture, username, title and/or “About You” section on my account?
    You can change your name, username, and title by clicking your profile picture in the top right corner and navigating to Your Profile. At the right corner, click Edit Profile and make changes here.
  • How can I reset my password?
    You can change your password by clicking your profile picture in the top right corner and navigating to Account Settings. Under Account, you will see Reset Password. Type in your current password and your new password and click Reset Password.
