Getting Started

  • What is UniHive?
    UniHive is a 100% free social platform for Black and people of color MBA enthusiasts at all stages in the process from prospective students to current students to alumni. Members can post/answer questions, start discussions, do pulse-checks and get connected with fellow peers! Join the Hive.
  • How do I join UniHive?
    You can sign up for UniHive here via computer, laptop, tablet, or phone. An email address is required.
  • How do I navigate the UniHive platform?
    Your Feed Your Feed is a central place of activity on UniHive, just like a timeline! From here, you can:
    • Create posts
    • Answer questions
    • Share files (like resumes and cover letters)
    • Interact with other UniHive members
    Your Feed allows you to post your thoughts to the main feed which sends it to all UniHive members or to your specific Communities. You choose!
    Your Profile This is your space to share what you want the Hive to know about you! Learn more here.
    Communities When you join UniHive, you will automatically become a member of the Hive. In true bee fashion, this is where you will find every member on the platform. You can access the Hive on the left side bar on Your Feed. Feel free to write a message introducing yourself to the Hive and you will be greeted right back! You can create a post via the post module above your home feed. Whether you are a silent bee or a busy one, we are excited to have you here!
    Search Find your UniHive friends really quickly or an old post by using UniHive’s search bar.
    Messaging You can send direct messages to UniHive members by clicking the message icon. Type and click a member's name in the search bar on the left and start writing your message at the bottom of your screen. Do not worry about whether or not you are following the person you are messaging. You can message anyone on the platform.
    Once you write your message, click that member’s icon. A member’s information panel will pop up on the right side of the screen where you have the option of following that member.

Your Account - Profile

  • How do I get to my profile?
    Click your profile picture in the top right corner and navigate to Profile.
  • How do I edit my profile?
    From your profile, click Edit Profile. You can do the following here:
    • Change your full name, username, title
    • Change your profile picture and banner image. The recommended minimum size is 1600 x 300 pixels for the banner image.
    • Update your “About You” section
    • Delete your account
  • How can I edit my posts?
    Head to Profile and click Posts. On the left bottom corner of each post, click the options icon (...) > click Edit to make changes.
  • How do I follow or unfollow members?
    Head to Profile and click Followers on the right side. Click the Following button on any member and you are now unfollowing them. Want to quickly follow that member? Click the Follow button on that member and you are following them again.
  • I forgot my password. Help!
    At the login page, click Forgot password? located next to the password box. Type in your email or username and you will receive a reset password email at your respective email address.

Your Account - Settings

  • How do I get to my account settings?
    Click your profile picture and navigate to Account Settings. You can do the following here:
    • Change your email address
    • Reset your password
    • Change the frequency of your notifications
