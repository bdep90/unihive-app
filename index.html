Inclusion

With less than 10% of Black students enrolled at business schools nation-wide, we recognize the need to create a social platform that equalizes the playing field.



Why UniHive?

Just like a beehive, UniHive fosters community building by making resource sharing easy, fast, and accessible. Whether you are at the start of your MBA journey or an alumni seeking mentorship, users are encouraged to sustain relationships with one another and change their realities. Isolation is no longer the only alternative! We’re here to provide a supportive, friendly, safe space for users.

Join UniHive

Our Story

After encountering mental fatigue, a lean support system, and inadequate resources while pursuing a degree, one of us remembered a valuable lesson: turn to your family. As sisters, we are our strongest and longest lived support network. Unfortunately, our Black and Latin American peers experience similar entry barriers to the MBA procress. UniHive is our solution to this problem.
