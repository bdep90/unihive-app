Inclusion

With less than 10% of Black students enrolled at business schools nation-wide, we recognize the need to create a social platform that equalizes the playing field.



Why UniHive?

Just like a beehive, UniHive fosters community building by making resource sharing easy, fast, and accessible. Whether you are at the start of your MBA journey or an alumni seeking mentorship, users are encouraged to sustain relationships with one another and change their realities. Isolation is no longer the only alternative! We’re here to provide a supportive, friendly, safe space for users.

Join UniHive

Four years ago, one of us was a college grad with her fourth job under her belt and aspirations to be an entrepreneur. However, she wasn't prepared for the mental, emotional, and financial stress of decoding and starting the MBA process. Unfortunately, our Black and Latin American peers experience similar entry barriers. As Black female founders and sisters, we understand firsthand how impactful it is to have a network of willing, knowledgeable, and helpful people to encourage and guide you through the process. UniHive is our commitment to Black and POC MBA enthusiasts.
