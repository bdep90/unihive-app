Why UniHive?

Like a beehive, UniHive fosters community building by making resource sharing easy, fast, and accessible. Whether you're at the start of your MBA journey or an alum seeking mentorship, we encourage you to make new connections and own your success. We’re here to provide a supportive, friendly, and safe space for members.

Our Story

A few years ago, one of us was a college grad with her fourth job under her belt and aspirations to be an entrepreneur. However, she wasn't prepared for the mental, emotional, and financial stress of decoding and starting the MBA process. Unfortunately, our colleagues and peers experience similar entry barriers.

As Black female founders and sisters, we understand firsthand how impactful it is to have a network of willing, knowledgeable, and helpful people to encourage and guide you through the process. UniHive is our commitment to Black and POC MBA enthusiasts.
