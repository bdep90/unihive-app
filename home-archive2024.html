Our Mission

Like a beehive, UniHive fosters community building by making resource sharing easy, fast, and accessible. Whether you're at the start of your MBA journey or an alum seeking mentorship, we encourage you to make new connections and own your success. We’re here to provide a supportive, friendly, and safe space.

Our Story

A few years ago, one of us was a college grad with her fourth job under her belt and aspirations to be an entrepreneur. However, she wasn't prepared for the mental, emotional, and financial stress of decoding and starting the MBA process. Unfortunately, our colleagues and peers experience similar entry barriers.

As Black female founders and sisters, we understand firsthand how impactful it is to have a network of willing, knowledgeable, and helpful people to encourage and guide you through the process. UniHive is our commitment to Black MBA enthusiasts.

You Make It Possible

100% of online donations will go towards scholarships and grants for our UniHive community.

Everyone should have access to knowledge and resources to achieve their goals. UniHive is providing that. We want to take things a step further and create scholarships, grants, and giveaways to support our community at every step of the way. With your help today, we can provide laptops, waivers for applications, books, supplies, stipends and so much more. Your donation will immediately impact our community in a major way. Thank you!
