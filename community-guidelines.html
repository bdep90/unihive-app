Community Guidelines

Welcome to UniHive!

We’re excited to have you join the Hive as you navigate your way through the MBA process or celebrate your successes. At UniHive, we’re committed to promoting connectivity and sharing knowledge about all MBA-related things. However, we have to make sure that everyone’s actions align with our purpose, and we respect community accountability. Here’s how we’re doing things at UniHive.

Do

  • Celebrate Big Wins & Waggle! - Waggle is a dance performed by bees to point out the location of flowers. In the spirit of bees celebrating their wins, we encourage emojis, gifs, shout outs or LARGE CAPS with exclamations to show your support. Interested in joining our community? Sign up and start buzzing.
  • Lead with Kindness - Practice kindness when entering conversations or reading content. Everyone entering this community may not share the same opinion as you and we welcome authenticity and openness. We recognize each person as a master of their experience and we want to respect each person’s journey in the Hive.
  • Maintain Privacy - We respect each individual's privacy and understand that it takes time to operate with trust on this platform. All happenings on the UniHive platform should remain on this platform. We’re creating a safe space.
  • Share - We welcome updates and check-ins on how you’re doing. We want this space to be lively and supportive. That becomes possible when Hive members share their experiences and forge bonds.

Don't

  • Post Promotions or Spam - We will not allow spamming, posting promotional material or posting links to inappropriate third party websites in our group. We reserve the right to delete comments at our discretion and block any repeat offenders. We will remove content that is fraudulent, deceptive or misleading.
  • Engage in Hate Speech or Bullying - Personal attacks, name-calling, impersonations, trolling and abuse will not be tolerated. We don’t allow defamatory, indecent, offensive, profane, discriminatory, misleading, unlawful or threatening comments. This includes any references related to race, age, gender, gender expression, religion, physical appearance, or any other personal identifier of an individual. UniHive is not about this at all.

In the event of failing to follow the community guidelines, UniHive will address all offenses. We reserve the right to remove all harmful content. If any individual commits an infraction, we can exercise a number of options from temporary suspension of an account to permanent removal from the platform.

If you notice any harmful activity and/or experience abuse, harassment, discrimination, or feel unsafe, please contact us.

You Make It Possible

100% of online donations will go towards scholarships and grants for our UniHive community.

Everyone should have access to knowledge and resources to achieve their goals. UniHive is providing that. We want to take things a step further and create scholarships, grants, and giveaways to support our community at every step of the way. With your help today, we can provide laptops, waivers for applications, books, supplies, stipends and so much more. Your donation will immediately impact our community in a major way. Thank you!

Forging bonds. Cultivating access.
